Where to go to find the restaurant

As stated previously, Pickle's Prehistoric Bites is located near the Safari Lodge on Isla Nublar. Since most people want to eat after the tour, there is a main pathway from the exit of the Tour to Pickle's Prehistoric Bites. Although there are other ways to get there, such as the bus or even more pathways from the Aviary. Just remember, if you ever get lost, conceal yourself behind some rocks. But watch out for leeches! A dedicated employee will search for you around an hour after your disappearance.

How to Confirm Your Reservation

Confirming a reservation is as easy as pie. All you have to do is call the number listed on the Reservations page, go to the link provided on the Reservations page, or go old school and make one in-person. When reserving a spot, please state if you need disability accommodations for seating. The only information you will need to provide is your name, a phone number, and a secondary email address. After all of that, you're all set and the last thing you have to do is show up!
