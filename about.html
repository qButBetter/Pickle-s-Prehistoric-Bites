How it Started

Our journey into the world of culinary exploration was deeply rooted in a shared fascination with exotic meats. The initial spark of inspiration came to us as we saw the first dinosaurs in Jurassic Park. The spectacle of these ancient creatures roaming the Earth ignited our imaginations and left us pondering the thrilling possibility of bringing a piece of that prehistoric world to the dining table. From that moment on, we were committed to offering a truly unique and immersive dining experience that allowed people to savor the flavors of exotic meats, and what could be more extraordinary than indulging in the culinary adventure of tasting dinosaur meat? It was this passion and ambition that led us to embark on the extraordinary journey of opening our restaurant, where we are proud to bring the concept of exotic dining to life.

Usual Experience

Although our restaurant is usually busy and reservation only, there are times that you can snag a table before the rush. The wait time to order appetizers ranges from Instant to 15 minutes at the most. Our servers are of the highest quality, so don't fear ill-manners. Our chefs are very efficient and will get your meal in a jiffy! If you wait more than 30 minutes total, you get a free unlimited side-dish! We also have entertainment in the establishment to keep the visit enjoyable. Such as dinosaur animatronics and "rain-storms" every half hour. Also, our mascot, Pickle the Velociraptor, comes out to entertain every 2 hours in the form of a performance similar to competing companies. So don't be late!
